Pre-interview Task

NISB Core Selection Process 2026

Task Description

Prepare a comprehensive annual budget plan for the entire NISB tenure in 2026, covering all fests, flagship events, and recurring activities conducted throughout the year.

Requirements

1. Full-Year Financial Plan

You are required to prepare a detailed financial plan that includes:

  • Estimated expenses for each fest and each flagship event
  • Budget allocation for recurring activities, regular meetings, and weekly/monthly engagements
  • Separate provisions for the following categories:
    • Marketing & promotions
    • Logistics & technical requirements
    • Hospitality & refreshments
    • Merchandise & giveaways
    • Contingency reserves

2. Breakdown & Structure

Your plan must include:

  • A clear breakdown of projected costs for every event and activity
  • Component-wise costing (e.g., marketing, logistics, hospitality)
  • Event-wise or month-wise structured tables
  • Clear assumptions or considerations used while determining estimates
  • Any additional notes that justify significant budgeting decisions

You may format the budget as:

  • ✓ A tabular event-wise breakdown
  • ✓ Month-wise calendar budget
  • ✓ A consolidated table with category-wise totals
  • ✓ Or any other clean and professional structure

3. Submission Expectations

Your budget plan should be submitted as a document (PDF, Excel, or Word format) and will be evaluated based on:

  • Realism and accuracy of cost estimates
  • Clarity and structure of tables and content
  • Professional formatting
  • Logical and justified assumptions
  • Completeness of the annual financial plan

You may use previous event trends, common student-branch expenses, or reasonable assumptions wherever necessary.
