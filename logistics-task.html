Pre-interview Task

NISB Core Selection Process 2026

Task Description

Prepare a detailed list of all logistics and materials that may be required throughout the 2026 tenure.

Required Categories

Your plan must cover all logistics and materials required throughout the year.

Examples include (but are not limited to):

  • Food & refreshments
  • Hoodies, T-shirts, and other merchandise
  • Stationery and printing materials
  • Goodies / giveaways (list any relevant options)
  • Event-specific materials and props
  • Any additional logistic items needed for smooth event execution

Note: These are examples to guide you. You are expected to identify and include ALL relevant logistics and materials that may be needed throughout the 2026 tenure.

For each item, you must also include:

  • Estimated budget (price range or approximate cost)
  • Possible vendors or shops from where these items can be purchased (local, online, or wholesale sources)

Submission Expectations

Your final submission should be:

  • Structured - Organized by categories or item types
  • Clear - Easy to read and understand
  • Professionally formatted - Use tables or section-wise format
  • Comprehensive - Include all relevant logistics items
  • Practical - Include realistic vendors and cost estimates

You may present your logistics plan in a table format or section-wise format. Submit as PDF, Excel (.xlsx), or Word (.docx) document.
